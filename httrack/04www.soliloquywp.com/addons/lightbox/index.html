Lightbox Addon Advanced

The Lightbox Addon is a complete integration of the prettyPhoto lightbox slider script into Soliloquy. This addon makes it incredibly easy to create lightbox images, photos and even galleries for your slider.

You can choose between lightbox slider images, videos, HTML, inline content, iframe content or even ajax content. You can select any type of theme provided by the script, and all of the options are configurable in the admin area for each slider. You get complete control over your lightboxes!
