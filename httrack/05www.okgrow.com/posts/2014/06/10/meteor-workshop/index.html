Our Meteor workshop was a success!

By Paul Dowman - June 10, 2014
Meteor, Events

Last weekend we held a two-day coding workshop where we showed how to build real-time JavaScript web apps using Meteor.

We thought it was a great success, and we got positive feedback. Here's what some of the participants said:

In a intimate and small setting, myself and other participants were able to have 1-on-1, hands-on coaching from Paul has his assistant Karl. This bootcamp has saved me the time I otherwise would have had to spend researching on my own.

- Apollo

The sessions were highly interactive, explorative, and almost like daily work in a dev shop all the while learning about Meteor. A great introduction to this emerging technology. +1

- Richard

Both Paul and Karl were very knowledgeable and took the time to help those who needed it, while still maintaining a comfortable pace. I came away with a good understanding of the basics of Meteor and an enthusiasm to put it to use in my own projects.

- Donna

We're going to do it again so if you're interested in learning Meteor ask us about it!
